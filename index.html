Know more about me

Hello! I’m Kate

With over 10 years of experience, my mission is to help end the struggle, to break free from dieting and battling your body. To stop second guessing, and to finally feel confident, stress-free, and in control of what and how you eat. The confusion, frustration and disappointment, ugh! You know it, I know it, diets don’t work. There’s a better way. Using a non-diet roadmap, I share the tools, skills, support, and knowledge to help you feel empowered and free in discovering what & how to eat, once and for all. I’m originally from Long Island, NY, have a degree in Dietetics from Miami of Ohio and earned a Master’s in Biological Nutrition Sciences from Long Island University CW Post. I live with my husband and two teenage daughters.
